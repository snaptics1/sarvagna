MND Avenue Plots in Medchal

MND Avenue plots in Medchal offer an exclusive lifestyle with top-notch amenities in a prime location.

Amenities That Define Excellence

Sarvagna Projects presents MND Avenue, a premium residential layout spread across 5 acres in Medchal. This HMDA-approved layout offers plots starting from 150 square yards, ensuring a modern and secure living environment. With excellent connectivity to the Nehru Outer Ring Road, Medchal Court, and NH-44, the project is strategically located near key landmarks like Malla Reddy Institutions, Medinova Super Speciality Hospital, and Kandlakoya IT Park, all within 10–15 minutes of driving distance.

MND Avenue provides well-planned plots with a focus on sustainability and quality. From avenue plantation to blacktop roads and 24/7 water and power supply, this project ensures a comfortable and peaceful lifestyle for residents. It's not just about purchasing land—it's an investment in a harmonious blend of nature, convenience, and luxury.
